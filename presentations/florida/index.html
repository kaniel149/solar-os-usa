Key Terminology

US vs Israel solar terms — essential glossary for market entry

AHJ
Authority Having Jurisdiction — local building dept (67 different ones in FL!)
PTO
Permission to Operate — utility says "turn it on." The finish line.
ITC
Investment Tax Credit — 30% federal tax credit (ending 2025 for residential)
NEM / Net Metering
1:1 retail rate credit for excess solar production. FL's key incentive.
Dealer Fee
15–37% added to loan balance by lender. Hidden cost. NO equivalent in Israel.
PE Stamp
Professional Engineer signature required on all FL solar permit plans.
NOA
Notice of Acceptance — product approval required in HVHZ (Miami-Dade/Broward)
HVHZ
High Velocity Hurricane Zone — Miami-Dade & Broward. 170–175 mph design.
Comp-out
Remove tiles, install composition shingle, mount solar on that. #1 FL method.
RSD
Rapid Shutdown Device — NEC 2020 requires ≤80V within 30 seconds.
FBC
Florida Building Code — stricter than most US states due to hurricanes.
CV License
Certified Solar Contractor — FL-specific license. 4 years experience required.
MSP / Panel Upgrade
Main Service Panel. Older FL homes need upgrade from 100A/150A to 200A ($1,500–3K)
One-Call Close (OCC)
Aggressive same-day contract signing. Common in US solar sales.
Clawback
Lender takes back dealer fee if customer cancels within X months.
Adder
Extra charge for complexity: tile roof adder, critter guard adder, etc.
HOA
Homeowners Association — 40–50% of FL homes. Cannot prohibit solar (F.S. 163.04)
EPC
Engineering, Procurement, Construction — the full project delivery company.
