FAQ

Common questions

How is Solar OS different from Aurora Solar?

Aurora is a design tool priced for large enterprises ($170+/user/mo). Solar OS is an all-in-one platform — CRM, proposals, design, permitting, and monitoring — built specifically for small-to-mid installers at a fraction of the cost. You replace 5-6 tools with one.

What states do you support?

We're launching in California, Texas, Florida, and New York with full AHJ permit databases and state-specific utility rate structures. We're expanding to all 50 states throughout 2026.

How does the AI proposal generator work?

Enter a property address and upload a utility bill. Our AI automatically sizes the system, calculates ITC savings (including bonus adders), generates cash/loan/lease/PPA comparisons, and produces a branded PDF proposal with e-signature — all in about 60 seconds.

Does it handle NEM 3.0 and battery storage?

Yes. Solar OS is NEM 3.0 aware for California projects. It automatically optimizes battery sizing based on TOU rates and includes Tesla Powerwall, Enphase IQ, and other battery options in proposals and designs.

What equipment brands are supported?

We're hardware-agnostic. Our database includes Enphase, SolarEdge, Tesla, REC, Q CELLS, Canadian Solar, Silfab, and more. You can also add custom equipment and pricing.

Can I migrate my data from other tools?

Absolutely. We offer free data migration from Salesforce, HubSpot, Aurora, and most common CRMs. Our team will help you get set up — most installers are fully migrated within 48 hours.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

No contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We also offer a 14-day free trial on all paid plans so you can test everything before committing.
