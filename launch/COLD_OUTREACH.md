# Cold Outreach Templates — Solar OS

Target: Florida solar company owner, 5-50 employees
Tone: Peer-to-peer. Kaniel = fellow solar company owner, not a software vendor.

---

## Email Template 1: Introduction (Cold)

**Subject:** Quick question about your permit workflow

**Body:**

Hi {FirstName},

I'm Kaniel — I run a solar installation company and recently built a tool to fix a problem that was driving me crazy: managing leads, permits, proposals, and monitoring across 5 different platforms.

I noticed {CompanyName} is active in {County} County — nice work. I pulled permits there too and know the process can be a pain.

I built Solar OS to put everything in one place — CRM, permit generator, proposal builder, and monitoring dashboard. One login, one monthly cost, no more copy-pasting between tabs.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week? I'd love to show you what we built and get your honest feedback.

Either way, keep crushing it out there.

Kaniel Tordjman
Founder, Solar OS
usasolaros.com

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, customize:
- [ ] {FirstName} — owner's first name
- [ ] {CompanyName} — their company
- [ ] {County} — county they operate in (check FL permit records)
- [ ] Email 3 data points — pull real numbers from permit databases

## Where to Find Prospects

1. **FL DBPR License Search** — Search "solar" under contractor licenses
2. **EnergySage installer directory** — Filter by Florida
3. **Google Maps** — "solar installer {city} FL"
4. **FLASEIA member directory** — After joining
5. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — Title: "Owner" + Industry: "Renewables" + Location: "Florida"
