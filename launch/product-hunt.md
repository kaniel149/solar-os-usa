# Product Hunt Launch — Solar OS

## Tagline (58 chars)
The all-in-one solar platform that replaces 6 tools at once

## Description (256 chars)
Solar OS is a CRM, proposal builder, project tracker, document manager, scheduling tool, and analytics dashboard — built specifically for residential solar installers. Stop paying $500/mo for 6 disconnected tools. One platform, one login, one price: $49/mo.

## 5 Key Features

⚡ **Instant Solar Proposals** — Generate professional proposals with system sizing, savings estimates, and financing options in under 60 seconds.

📊 **Visual Sales Pipeline** — Drag-and-drop CRM built for solar sales cycles. Track every lead from knock to install.

📁 **Smart Document Hub** — Permits, interconnection agreements, contracts — all organized per project. No more hunting through email.

📅 **Crew Scheduling** — Assign install crews, manage timelines, and avoid double-bookings with a calendar built for field work.

📈 **Real-Time Dashboard** — See close rates, revenue, average deal size, and crew utilization at a glance. Know your numbers without exporting CSVs.
