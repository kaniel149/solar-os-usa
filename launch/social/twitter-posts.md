## Tweet 7: Thread — Lessons (3 tweets)

**Tweet 7.1:**
5 things I learned building SaaS as a non-technical founder:

**Tweet 7.2:**
1. Domain expertise > coding skills (AI handles code, nobody can AI your 10 years in the field)

2. Talk to 20 customers before writing a line of code

3. Ship ugly. Ship fast. Let users tell you what matters

4. Vertical SaaS > horizontal SaaS for solo founders (smaller market = less competition = easier to win)

5. $49/mo from 400 users = $20K MRR

You don't need millions of users.

The math works if the product works.
