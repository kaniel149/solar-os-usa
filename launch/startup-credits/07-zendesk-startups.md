# Zendesk for Startups

## Program URL
https://www.zendesk.com/startups/

## What You Get
- 6 months free access to Zendesk products
- Up to 50 agents included
- Covers: Zendesk Support, Talk, Chat, Guide, Explore
- Access to Zendesk CX Academy
- Virtual office hours with Zendesk team
- Best practice guides and webinars

### NOT Included
- Consulting services
- Add-ons and integrations
- Zendesk Talk minutes

## Eligibility
- Brand new Zendesk customer (no existing or previous paid accounts)
- Fewer than 50 employees
- Raised VC funding up to Series A (Angel, Pre-seed, Seed, Series A, or Series B)

### Note on Funding Requirement
Zendesk requires some form of VC funding. As a self-funded startup, we may need to:
- Check if bootstrapped startups qualify (ask during application)
- Apply after receiving any form of angel investment
- Join an accelerator first

## Application Steps
1. Go to https://www.zendesk.com/startups/
2. Start a trial account to test products
3. Do NOT start a paid subscription before approval (credits only redeemable during trial)
4. Apply for the startup program
5. Wait for email with application status and next steps
6. If approved, 6-month free period begins
7. Before program end date, decide to continue paid or cancel

## What to Write in the Application

**Company Description:**
KANIEL TORD LLC is building Solar OS, an all-in-one SaaS platform for the solar energy industry. Our platform serves solar companies with CRM, project management, proposal generation, and installation tracking.

**Why Zendesk:**
As we scale Solar OS to serve multiple solar companies, we need enterprise-grade customer support:
- Multi-channel support (email, chat, phone) for solar company teams
- Knowledge base (Guide) for self-service documentation
- Analytics (Explore) for support quality metrics
- Scalable solution that grows with our customer base

## Priority: LOW-MEDIUM
- May not qualify without VC funding
- Consider after receiving any investment
- Intercom Early Stage may be better fit for us

## Estimated Value: ~$3,000-6,000 (6 months of Suite plans)
