https://mixpanel.com/startups-apply/

## What You Get
- **First year FREE** on exclusive Startup Plan (worth $150K+)
- 1 billion annual events
- 500K Session Replay recordings
- Unlimited team seats
- Advanced analytics (funnels, flows, retention, cohorts)
- World-class support
- After year 1: move to Free, Growth, or Enterprise plan

## Eligibility
- Incorporated less than 5 years ago
- Raised no more than $8M USD in total funding
- Not previously on a paid Mixpanel plan
- Not redeemed other Mixpanel offers
- Not previously participated in the program

## Important Rules
- Must start sending data to Mixpanel within **90 days** of acceptance (or risk removal, no rejoining)
- 1B events/year limit; exceeding may require upgrade
- No BAAs or MSAs available
- One-time program — cannot rejoin

## Application Steps
1. Go to https://mixpanel.com/startups-apply/
2. Fill out application form:

   - Funding amount
   - VC/accelerator referral (optional)
3. Submit application
4. Upon approval, Startup Plan activates immediately
5. **Start sending data within 90 days!**
6. Contact startups@mixpanel.com for questions
