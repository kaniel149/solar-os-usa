# Intercom Early Stage Program

## Program URL
https://www.intercom.com/early-stage

## What You Get
- **Year 1:** 90% off Advanced plan ($65/month instead of ~$650)
- **Year 2:** 50% off + 150 free Fin AI resolutions/month
- **Year 3:** 25% off + 75 free Fin AI resolutions/month
- Includes: 6 Advanced seats, 6 Copilot seats, 20 Lite seats, 500 messages/month for proactive support
- 300 free Fin AI resolutions/month in year 1

## Eligibility
- Raised up to $10 million in funding (we qualify — $0 raised)
- Fewer than 15 employees (we qualify — solo founder)
- Not currently an Intercom customer (new customers only)

## Application Steps
1. Go to https://www.intercom.com/early-stage
2. Fill out application
3. Wait 1-2 business days for approval
4. Once approved, you have 7 days to start subscription
5. If not activated within 30 days, application expires (must reapply)

## Important Notes
- One-time opportunity — once you leave for a standard plan, you cannot rejoin
- Usage-based charges (Fin resolutions beyond free, Phone, SMS, WhatsApp) are full price
- Can add more seats at same discount rate
- Cannot reduce below 6 included seats
