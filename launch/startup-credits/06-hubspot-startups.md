## Program URL
https://www.hubspot.com/startups

## What You Get
### With Partner Affiliation (VC/Accelerator)
- **Year 1:** 90% off Professional or Enterprise plans
- **Year 2:** 50% off
- **Year 3:** 25% off

### Bootstrap Program (no VC required — our path)
- **Year 1:** 30% off Professional or Enterprise plans
- URL: https://www.hubspot.com/startups/bootstrap-program

### Without Partner but with $2M+ raised
- **Year 1:** 50% off

## Eligibility
### Bootstrap Program (best fit for us)
- Less than $1M in revenue/funding
- Fewer than 10 employees
- Not a reseller or consultancy
- Early-stage startup

### Standard Program
- Pre-seed, Seed, or Series A
- Affiliated with approved HubSpot partner (VC, accelerator, incubator)
- Verified via Crunchbase/Pitchbook
