# Crisp Startup Program

## Program URL
https://crisp.chat (apply via live chat)
https://help.crisp.chat/en/article/how-to-apply-to-startup-program-lyfxt4/

## What You Get
- **30% lifetime discount** on Essentials or Plus plans
- No expiration — discount stays as long as you're a customer
- Applies to ongoing subscription

## Eligibility
- Less than $1M in funding (we qualify — $0)
- Company less than 3 years old (we qualify)
- New customer
- Must be on Essentials or Plus plan

## Application Steps
1. Sign up for a Crisp account (14-day free trial)
2. Start live chat on crisp.chat
3. Request the Startup Program
4. Provide required documents:
   - URL of your website (kanielt.com)
   - Short description of business and services
   - Business registration certificate (KANIEL TORD LLC Florida registration)
5. If approved, discount code applied immediately

## What to Write in Chat

> Hi! I'd like to apply for the Crisp Startup Program.
> 
> - Company: KANIEL TORD LLC
> - Website: kanielt.com
> - Description: We're building Solar OS, an all-in-one SaaS platform for the solar energy industry — CRM, project management, proposal generation, and installation tracking for solar companies.
> - Funding: Self-funded, less than $1M
> - Founded: [year] — less than 3 years old
> - I have our Florida LLC registration certificate available.

## Priority: LOW
- 30% off is nice but not game-changing
- Consider only if choosing Crisp over Intercom for customer support
