# Twilio Segment Startup Program

## Program URL
https://segment.com/startup-program/ (or via https://www.twilio.com/en-us/solutions/startups)

## What You Get
- Up to **$50,000 in free Segment credits** (Team Plan, 2 years)
- Over **$1M in additional partner credits** for integrations (AWS, Google Analytics, Amplitude, Intercom)
- Access to Segment University and Analytics Academy
- Priority support
- **Year 3:** 50% discount
- **Year 4:** 25% discount

## Eligibility
- Less than 24 months old
- Raised less than $5 million in total funding
- Not currently under a Segment sales contract
- **International exception:** Companies outside US/Canada/UK are exempt from age requirement if <20 employees and no sales contract

## Application Steps
1. Go to Segment startup program page
2. Fill out application:
   - Company name: KANIEL TORD LLC
   - Location: Florida, USA
   - Age: [months since incorporation]
   - Funding: $0 (self-funded)

3. Submit application
