# FLASEIA Membership Plan — Solar OS

## What is FLASEIA?

Florida Solar Energy Industries Association — the state-level trade association for solar professionals in Florida. Every serious FL solar company is a member or knows about it. It's THE networking hub for the FL solar market.

Website: https://www.flaseia.org
Join: https://members.flaseia.org/join

## How to Join

1. Go to https://members.flaseia.org/join
2. Select membership category (Consultant or Contractor)
3. Choose tier level
4. Fill out company info + payment
5. Membership is annual, auto-renews

## Membership Cost

| Category | Tier | Estimated Annual Cost |
|----------|------|----------------------|
| Consultant | Base | ~$765/year |
| Contractor | Base | ~$765/year |
| Contractor | Platinum | ~$5,000-$15,950/year |
| Friends of FlaSEIA | Individual/Consumer | $25/year |

**Recommendation:** Start with **Consultant — Base tier** (~$765/year). This gets you in the door with full member benefits. Upgrade to Platinum later once Solar OS has revenue to justify the extra visibility (homepage features, social media exposure).

**Note:** 25% of dues may NOT be tax-deductible (used for lobbying). The rest is deductible as a business expense for KANIEL TORD LLC.

## Networking Strategy

### Before Joining
- [ ] Follow FLASEIA on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook
- [ ] Engage with their content (comment on posts, share articles)
- [ ] Identify 10 committee members to connect with on LinkedIn

### After Joining
- [ ] Join Marketing & Events Committee (natural fit for a SaaS founder)
- [ ] Join Education Committee (position Solar OS as an industry educator)
- [ ] Attend first "Beyond the Summit" local event within 60 days
- [ ] Introduce yourself to the Executive Director — explain Solar OS, ask how to contribute

### At Events
- [ ] Lead with "I run a solar company" not "I sell software"
- [ ] Ask questions first: "What's your biggest operational headache?"
- [ ] Offer the demo only after they express a pain point
- [ ] Collect business cards → follow up within 48 hours with personalized email
- [ ] Post event photos on LinkedIn, tag FLASEIA and people you met
