## Solar OS Launches All-in-One Platform to Replace the 6+ Disconnected Tools Solar Installers Are Forced to Use

*Israeli solar veteran builds affordable alternative to enterprise software — from a laptop in Thailand*

---

**MIAMI, FL — [LAUNCH DATE] —** KANIEL TORD LLC today announced the public launch of **Solar OS**, an all-in-one software platform designed to replace the fragmented technology stack that plagues small and mid-size solar installation companies across the United States.

Solar OS combines CRM, AI-powered proposal generation, solar and battery system design, jurisdiction-aware permit package automation, and unified fleet monitoring into a single platform — starting at $49 per user per month, roughly 70% less than competing enterprise solutions.

### The Problem: A $50 Billion Industry Running on Spreadsheets

The US residential solar market is projected to exceed $50 billion annually, with over 30% year-over-year growth driven by Inflation Reduction Act incentives. Yet the software serving this industry remains fragmented and overpriced.

The average small solar installer — typically a 1-50 person company responsible for the majority of residential installations in America — juggles between 5 and 8 disconnected software tools: one for customer relationship management, another for system design, a third for proposals, separate systems for permitting and project management, individual monitoring portals for each inverter brand, and spreadsheets to fill the gaps.

The leading design tool, Aurora Solar, charges $170+ per seat per month — pricing that effectively excludes the small shops that form the backbone of America's solar workforce.

"I ran a solar installation company for over a decade," said **Kaniel Tordjman**, founder of Solar OS. "The technology situation never improved. We'd design in one tool, generate proposals in another, track permits in a spreadsheet, and monitor systems by logging into four different inverter portals. Every installer I know has this exact same problem. I built Solar OS because I got tired of waiting for someone else to fix it."

### Key Features

- **Integrated Solar CRM:** Full lead-to-Permission-to-Operate (PTO) pipeline with US lead source integration
- **60-Second AI Proposals:** Address and utility bill input generates complete proposals with ITC calculations, financing comparisons, and e-signature-ready PDF export
- **AHJ-Aware Permit Generator:** Automated permit package generation that maps requirements across thousands of US jurisdictions and integrates with SolarAPP+ for instant approvals
- **Solar + Battery Design Engine:** Hardware-agnostic system design with NEM 3.0 awareness, TOU optimization, and a comprehensive US equipment database
- **Unified Monitoring Dashboard:** All inverter brands (Enphase, SolarEdge, Huawei) consolidated into a single fleet view with automated alert systems

### The Permitting Breakthrough

Solar OS's permit automation represents a significant advance for the industry. Permitting inconsistency across America's thousands of local jurisdictions — known as Authorities Having Jurisdiction (AHJs) — is widely cited as the single largest bottleneck in residential solar installation, often extending simple projects from weeks to months.

Solar OS maintains a database of AHJ-specific requirements and auto-generates compliant permit packages including site plans, single-line diagrams, and structural calculations. Integration with SolarAPP+ enables instant permit approvals in participating jurisdictions.

Early users report reducing permit package assembly time from 5-10 hours to under 30 minutes per project.
